Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: we do not store your lab results. Your health data is processed in real time and never saved to our servers. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Information we collect

When you use the AgelessLabs AI Biomarker Interpreter, any lab results you upload or paste are sent directly to our AI provider for analysis and are not stored on our servers. We do not retain, log, or save your health data in any form.

We may collect the following non-personal, anonymous usage data:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Browser type and device type
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Referral source (how you found the site)

2. How we use information

Anonymous usage data is used solely to understand how people use AgelessLabs and to improve the product. It is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to identify individual users.

3. Your health data

Any lab results you submit are processed in real time by our AI and returned to you immediately. We do not store, log, or retain this data after your session ends. We strongly recommend you do not include your name, date of birth, or other personal identifiers in any data you submit.

4. Third party services

AgelessLabs uses the following third party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your lab data for AI analysis. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosts our website. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — serves typography. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

We may earn affiliate commissions when you click links to third party lab testing services. This does not affect your cost or our editorial independence.

5. Cookies

We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics that do not require cookies or personal data. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Continued use of AgelessLabs after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@agelesslabs.ai
