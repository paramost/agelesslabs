About

Longevity intelligence
for everyone.

Most people receive their blood test results as a list of numbers with no context. AgelessLabs exists to change that — giving anyone access to the kind of deep biomarker interpretation that was previously only available to patients of expensive longevity clinics.

The problem we're solving

Standard reference ranges on blood tests are designed to detect disease — not to optimize for longevity. A result marked "normal" by your doctor may still be far from the range associated with a long, healthy life. Most people have no way of knowing the difference.

At the same time, the science of longevity has advanced rapidly. We now know which biomarkers matter most for healthspan, what optimal ranges look like, and what interventions actually move the needle. That knowledge exists — but it's locked inside specialist clinics, academic papers, and expensive concierge medicine programs.

What AgelessLabs does

AgelessLabs is an AI-powered platform that interprets your blood test results through a longevity lens. Upload your labs — as a PDF, an image, or pasted text — and our AI analyzes every marker against longevity-optimized ranges, generates a personalized score, and tells you exactly what to do next.

It's not a replacement for your doctor. It's the context your doctor doesn't have time to give you — and the second opinion you've always wanted.

Our principles

Accuracy first
We interpret biomarkers based on the best available longevity science — not commercial incentives or outdated reference ranges.
Radical transparency
We explain every recommendation. You should always know why a marker matters and what the evidence says.
Privacy by design
Your health data is yours. We don't store your lab results and never will.
Accessible longevity
The science of living longer shouldn't be a luxury. Our core tool is free and always will be.
